Frequently Asked Questions

To record a dream, navigate to the Dreams page and click the "Record Dream" button. Fill in the details including the title, date, time, emotions, and description. You can also add audio recordings and tags to organize your dreams better.

Go to the Prayers page and click "Add Prayer". Enter your prayer request, add relevant tags, and set whether it's private or public. You can also enable reminders to help you remember to pray regularly.

Custom emotions allow you to create personalized emotion tags beyond the default ones. Go to Settings > Custom Emotions to create your own emotions with custom names, emojis, and colors to better express your feelings.

When viewing a prayer, click the "Mark as Answered" button. You can add updates to track the journey of your prayer and celebrate when it's answered. All answered prayers are displayed on your Praise Wall.

Yes! When recording a dream or prayer, you can either upload an audio file or record directly using your device's microphone. This is great for capturing your thoughts immediately after waking up or during prayer time.

When viewing a dream or prayer entry, click the delete button (trash icon). You'll be asked to confirm the deletion to prevent accidental removal. Note that deleted entries cannot be recovered.
